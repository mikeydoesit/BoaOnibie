Boa Onibie

Kingdom FM's broadcasting stations are raising funds to support orphanages and those in need this year. ‘Boa Onibie’ - twi for 'help the have nots' - aims at bringing happiness to orphaned children and feeding and clothing the poor in society during Christmas. The programme forms part of Unique Kingdom Communications broader commitment to social responsibility.
